About Thrissur

Thrissur, formerly known as Trichur, is a city in the state of Kerala, India. It is known as the "Cultural Capital of Kerala" due to its rich history, cultural heritage, and religious diversity.

Thrissur is famous for its temples, churches, festivals, and vibrant cultural traditions. The city has a unique blend of traditional and modern elements, making it a popular destination for tourists and pilgrims.

Culture

The culture of Thrissur is deeply rooted in its religious traditions, art, music, and festivals. The city is renowned for its classical art forms such as Kathakali, Mohiniyattam, and Theyyam.

Thrissur Pooram is the most famous festival celebrated in the city, attracting thousands of visitors from around the world. It is a grand spectacle featuring processions of decorated elephants, traditional music, and fireworks.
